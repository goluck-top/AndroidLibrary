源码地址：[LoadingButtonAndroid](https://github.com/leandroBorgesFerreira/LoadingButtonAndroid) 文档：[doc](https://github.com/leandroBorgesFerreira/LoadingButtonAndroid/blob/master/READEME_CN.md)
